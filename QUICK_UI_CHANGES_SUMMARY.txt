USER WORKFLOW COMPARISON
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

BEFORE (To record activity):
1. Click phone navbar "Record Activity" button
2. GPS recorder opens
3. Record activity
4. Go back to Training to see in calendar

AFTER (To record activity):
1. Go to Training page
2. Click "Record Activity" blue button (top section)
3. GPS recorder opens
4. Record activity
5. Activity automatically shows in Training page

Benefits:
✅ Fewer navigation steps
✅ Activity visible immediately in context
✅ Better visual feedback
✅ Faster workflow
