## Data Refresh Timing

```
Action: Save GPS Activity
           ↓ (0 ms)
Database: 3 tables updated
           ↓ (50-100 ms)
Client: Pages automatically refresh
           ↓ (200 ms max)
UI: Activity visible in all locations
```

**Real-time:** Changes appear within 1 second of saving

---

## Summary of Locations

| Location | Page | What Shows | Updates |
|----------|------|-----------|---------|
| Training | Calendar | Activity on date | Instantly |
| Performance | Heatmap | Counted in activity total | Instantly |
| Activity History | List + Map | Full details + route | Instantly |
| Athlete Dashboard | Today's Activities | Quick summary | Instantly |
| Training Page | Daily View | Expanded details | Instantly |

---

## Important Notes

✅ **No Duplicate Display:** Each activity appears once per view (Strava + GPS shown separately)
✅ **Source Identification:** Always marked as "Asciende GPS" to distinguish from Strava
✅ **Unified Analytics:** Heatmap counts all activity types equally
✅ **Privacy Respected:** Private activities only visible to you + assigned trainers
✅ **Editable:** Can update notes, RPE, and tags after saving

---

## Your First GPS Activity

1. Record activity (Run/Bike/Swim) using ActivityRecorder
2. Press "Save Activity"
3. You'll immediately see it:
   - ✅ In Training calendar
   - ✅ In Performance heatmap (next refresh)
   - ✅ In Activity History page
   - ✅ In map view (click "View Map")

**Congratulations! Your GPS data is now part of your complete training profile.**
